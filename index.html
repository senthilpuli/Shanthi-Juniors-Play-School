About the Institution

Building the Foundation
of a Brilliant Future

Shanthi Juniors Play School is a distinguished early childhood institution committed to delivering world-class foundational education. Our approach combines structured academics with experiential learning, ensuring every child develops the skills, confidence, and character needed for lifelong success.

Safe & Secure Environment

CCTV-monitored premises, trained staff, and rigorous safety protocols ensure complete security for every child.

Activity-Based Learning

Our curriculum integrates hands-on exploration with structured lessons, supporting deep conceptual understanding.

Holistic Development Focus

Equal emphasis on cognitive, emotional, social, and physical development across all age groups.

Certified & Experienced Faculty

All educators hold recognized early childhood certifications and undergo continuous professional development.
